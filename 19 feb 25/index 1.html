Traffic Management Solutions

DESCRIPTION

TrafficMon® is a comprehensive suite of traffic law enforcement and monitoring solutions that utilizes the power of AI and Machine Learning. It provides a range of capabilities for detecting violations, including Red light jump, Over Speeding, No Helmet riding, Triple Riding, Wrong Lane movement, and various other traffic violations in real-time. Additionally, TrafficMon® offers features such as traffic flow monitoring, Intelligent Traffic Management System (ITMS), and Traffic Management System to enhance overall traffic management. With its advanced technologies and functionalities, TrafficMon® is a valuable tool for ensuring road safety and efficient transportation management with Intelligent Transport Management System.
