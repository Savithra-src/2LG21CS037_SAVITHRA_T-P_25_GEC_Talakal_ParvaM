HOSTEL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

A hostel management system is a software application designed to streamline and automate various aspects of managing a hostel, including student admissions, room allocation, fee payments, mess management, visitor logs.


The security monitoring essentially providing a centralized platform to efficiently handle all administrative tasks related to student accommodation, eliminating the need for manual record-keeping and simplifying processes for hostel wardens and staff.
